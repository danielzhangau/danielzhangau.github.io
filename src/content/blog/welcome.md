Hi, I'm Daniel — an AI/ML engineer based in Brisbane, Australia. Over the past 4+ years I've been building production AI systems: computer vision pipelines for road and waste monitoring deployed across Australian councils, LLM-powered IoT analytics platforms, and more recently, domain-specific business chatbots.

This blog is where I share what I learn along the way.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Production ML lessons** — Real challenges from shipping computer vision and LLM systems to production: data pipelines, model serving, failure modes, and the engineering work that doesn't make it into research papers
- **Technical deep-dives** — Explorations of architectures, tools, and patterns I use daily — from object detection tuning to LLM function calling orchestration
- **Industry landscape** — How the AI/ML field is evolving and what it means for engineers building real systems

## Why Write?

Two reasons. First, writing forces clarity — if I can't explain something concisely, I don't understand it well enough. Second, the AI field moves fast and I want a personal knowledge base I can reference when the next wave of models and frameworks arrives.

If you find any of it useful, that's a bonus. Feel free to [reach out](/#contact) — I'm always happy to discuss AI engineering.
